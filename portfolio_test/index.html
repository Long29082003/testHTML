About me

I spent my career seeking for the beauty lurking beneath what is called the mundane in the everyday. I believe that every second in life is precious and brimmed with brilliant moments that, unfortunately, those ephemeral and delicate frames usually left unnoticed by the unceassant flow of time and the bustling of modern life. To that light, I set out my journey to seek the beauty in the moment, to eternalize of what had passed, and to materialize that intangible beauty under the lens of my camera.

As the famous saying in my country goes: "tui la cuc cut", which means I am a box of chocolate, I embrace life with the most ardent heart and always strive to set foot to experience new things and traverse new horizons.
